FAQ & Guidelines

How to report a found item

  1. Go to Report Item and scan the QR on the found item (or upload a QR photo).
  2. Fill in your name, the location where you found it, and take or attach a photo of the item.
  3. Submit — staff will verify and the item will appear in the Lost Items list.

How to claim an item

  1. Find your item in the Lost Items section and click “Claim Item”.
  2. Enter the email address you used when you registered the item.
  3. Submit — staff will verify and mark the item as returned to you.

QR Tagging instructions

Attach the generated QR sticker to the item in a visible spot. The QR contains a unique ID that links to the owner and item info.
